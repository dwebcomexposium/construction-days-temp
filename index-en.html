Over the space of three days in Lyon, located at the crossroads of Europe and in the heart of a key region and an unavoidable market for building and public works contractors, CONSTRUCTION DAYS will offer an unprecedented platform for the construction and infrastructure sector.

A full-scale replica of a worksite, giving centre stage to innovation.

CONSTRUCTION DAYS will allow the sector’s leading players to showcase their innovative solutions on the replica of a construction site through 5 hubs of expertise:

  • Earthmoving, Demolition & Transportation
  • Roads, Minerals & Foundations
  • Buildings & Concrete sector
  • Lifting & Handling
  • New technologies

100% live demonstrations
100% targeted contacts
100% prospective content

Your opportunity to present, in real-life use conditions, all the functions, performances and innovation of your equipment and solutions. Visitors will enjoy a memorable live experience in a convivial atmosphere!

10,000 specifiers, buyers and users of machinery will have access to:

  • demonstration rings,
  • a vast outdoor exhibition area,
  • and an indoor exhibition space.

Among the show’s highlights:

  • the organisation of business meetings between exhibitors and targeted visitors.
  • the organisation of a used equipment auction.
  • a series of talks and workshops to gain insight into the challenges and issues of the construction world of tomorrow.
